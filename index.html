Colour Experiments

Part 1 is experimenting with colours using the p5.js library, November 18, 2018

First part of my Creative Coding studies is manipulating colours in many ways but in a controlled manner. The ability to directly influence millions of colours gives users an amazing freedom. In this chapter I developed a step by step progression of the Colour sketeches from the Generative-gestaltung website. Here we manipulate colours by contrasting colours or creating a controlled randomness

Generative Design

Generative design is a method of creating artwork, models, and animations with programmed algorithms, has in recent years become a popular tool for designers. By using simple languages such as JavaScript in p5.js, artists and makers can create everything from interactive typography and textiles to 3D-printed furniture to complex and elegant infographics.
